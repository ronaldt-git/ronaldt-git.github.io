Steady Linguist

Steady Linguist is a Chrome extension that passively helps you learn a new language by translating a small percentage of words on every webpage you visit. You can control how much text is translated, pick your target language, and gradually absorb vocabulary in context — all while browsing normally.

It's simple, subtle, and effective. Whether you're a beginner or brushing up on fluency, Steady Linguist helps you reinforce learning without interrupting your routine.
