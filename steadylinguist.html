Steady Linguist

Steady Linguist is a Chrome extension built on the proven method of passive language acquisition idea that exposure to small, consistent doses of a new language throughout your daily routine leads to stronger retention and natural learning over time.

By translating a subtle percentage of the words you read online, Steady Linguist helps you pick up vocabulary in context without interrupting your workflow. It’s like immersion, but integrated directly into your browser.
