Privacy Policy for Steady Linguist

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

Steady Linguist is a Chrome extension developed by Outleveled Games that helps users passively learn new languages by translating a small percentage of visible text on web pages.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from users while using the extension itself.

However, if you sign in via Google, we collect your email address for account management, subscription tracking, and usage syncing via Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your settings and preferences across devices
  • To track translation usage for free and premium tier enforcement
  • To notify you of new features or important updates (if opted-in)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) – for authentication, database storage, and usage tracking
  • Stripe – for managing premium subscription payments
  • LibreTranslate – for performing translations on text snippets

These services may independently collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security

All user data transmitted is encrypted via HTTPS and securely stored using Firebase and Stripe industry-standard safeguards.

5. Your Choices

You may uninstall the extension at any time and can request deletion of your synced account data by emailing outleveledgames@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. We will post any changes to this page and may notify signed-in users via the extension if the changes are significant.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at outleveledgames@gmail.com.
