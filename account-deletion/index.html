Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data for one or more of our titles, email support@outleveledgames.com with the subject line "{Game Title} Account Deletion Request".

What To Include

  • Game title (for example: Operation Zombie, Runeforged Heroes)
  • Player ID (PlayFab ID), if available
  • Any additional details that help us locate your account, such as your display name or a linked email address

What Happens Next

After we verify your request, we will delete your account and associated data except for any information we must retain for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
