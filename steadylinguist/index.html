Steady Linguist

Steady Linguist is a Chrome extension built around passive language acquisition: small, consistent exposure to a new language during your normal browsing routine.

By translating a subtle percentage of the words you read online, Steady Linguist helps you pick up vocabulary in context without interrupting your workflow. It's like immersion built directly into your browser.
