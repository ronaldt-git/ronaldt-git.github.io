Steady Linguist

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2025

This policy covers the Steady Linguist Chrome extension and related premium account features. It may be updated as the extension evolves.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store sensitive personal information just from passive extension use. However, if you sign in via Google, we may process your email address for account management, subscription tracking, and syncing.

2. How We Use Information

  • Sync settings and preferences across devices.
  • Track usage for free-tier and premium-tier enforcement.
  • Provide support and account-related service communication where needed.

3. Third-Party Services

Steady Linguist currently uses or may rely on the following services:

  • Firebase (Google) for authentication, database storage, and usage tracking.
  • Stripe for premium subscription payments.
  • LibreTranslate for translation processing.

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

Data transmitted for account and premium features is intended to be sent over HTTPS and stored using provider safeguards. No system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You may uninstall the extension at any time. You may also request deletion of synced account data by emailing outleveledgames@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. If we make material changes, we may post the update here or communicate it through the product where appropriate.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, contact outleveledgames@gmail.com.
